## Shipping 

If you have any questions regarding shipping/tracking your order, we recommend reaching out to the merchant directly. We do not oversee the inventory and shipping processes for merchants. 

## Product Questions 

If you receive a damaged or defective product or have questions about using the product you purchased, we recommend reaching out to the merchant directly. The merchant will be best suited to support the returns or replacement process. Additionally, the merchant will be able to answer product specific questions you may have.
