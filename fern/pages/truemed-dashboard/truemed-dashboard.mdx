---
title: Truemed Dashboard
description: How to access and use your Truemed customer dashboard to view qualifications, LMNs, order history, and account settings.
---

Your Truemed dashboard is the central place to manage your HSA/FSA qualifications, download your Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN), and review past purchases. This article walks through everything available to you once you log in.

## Accessing your customer dashboard

To access your dashboard, go to [truemed.com](https://www.truemed.com) and click **Log In** in the upper right corner of the page.

You will need the email address you used when you completed your Truemed qualification survey or checkout flow. This is typically the same email associated with your purchase from the merchant. If you are unsure which email you used, check your inbox for a confirmation or survey email from Truemed.

Once logged in, you will land on your dashboard, which gives you access to:

- Your active and past qualifications (LMNs)
- Your order history linked to Truemed
- Your account settings

## Viewing your qualifications

The qualifications section of your dashboard lists every LMN that has been issued to you. For each qualification, you will see:

- **Merchant** -- the store or brand associated with the purchase
- **Product or category** -- the item or health category that was qualified
- **Status** -- whether the LMN is active or expired
- **Issue date** -- when the LMN was generated

To download your LMN, click the download icon or the **Download PDF** button next to the relevant qualification. The LMN is a PDF document you can save to your device or share with your benefits administrator or FSA/HSA provider if they request documentation.

Truemed no longer sends LMNs by email. All documents are accessed securely through your dashboard to protect your personal health information.

## LMN status & history

Each LMN in your dashboard will show one of the following statuses:

- **Active** -- the LMN is current and valid
- **Expired** -- the LMN has passed its validity period; a new qualification may be needed for future purchases
- **Pending** -- the LMN is still being processed; check back within 48 hours

To view a past LMN, scroll through your qualifications list. All previously issued documents remain accessible in your dashboard even after they expire.

**If an LMN is missing from your dashboard:**

1. Confirm you are logged in with the exact email you used to complete the qualification survey.
2. Wait up to 48 hours after completing the survey -- it can take time for your order to be processed.
3. Try logging in using an incognito or private browser window to rule out a caching issue.
4. If it still does not appear, contact [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) with your full name and the approximate date you completed the survey.

## Order history

Your order history shows purchases that have been linked to a Truemed qualification. For each order, you can view:

- The merchant name
- The purchase date
- The order amount
- The associated LMN, if one was issued

To access a receipt for a specific purchase, you will need to return to the original merchant's website or check the confirmation email you received at the time of purchase. Truemed's order history section reflects the qualification record, not a full transaction receipt.

## Account settings

In your account settings, you can update the following:

- **Display name** -- the name shown on your account profile
- **Password** -- update your login password at any time

**To change the email address on your account**, you will need to contact [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com). Email changes require verification to protect account security and cannot be made directly from the dashboard.

If you have questions about notification preferences or need help with anything not covered here, reach out to the Truemed support team at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com).
