### Refund Policy for HSA/FSA Cards

- **Refunds can only go back to the original payment method.** If you paid using an HSA or FSA card, the funds must be returned to that same card.
- **We cannot issue refunds to a different card, issue a check, or reprocess a refund to a new account.** This is a compliance requirement under IRS rules and enforced by our payment processor.
***
### What If My HSA/FSA Account Is Closed?

If your account has been closed and the card is no longer active:

- The refund will still be sent to that account.
- You will need to work directly with your HSA/FSA provider to retrieve the funds.
***
### Need Help?

If you’re unsure whether a refund has been issued or you don’t see the funds after 10 business days, reach out to us at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com). We’re happy to confirm the status of your refund.
