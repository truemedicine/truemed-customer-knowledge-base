title: "Is Peloton HSA_FSA Eligible_"---


### **Overview**

Peloton, in partnership with Truemed, offers a pathway for select HSA/FSA plan holders to use their funds toward Peloton hardware and potentially membership fees. Below is how the process typically works — please check with your HSA/FSA administrator to confirm eligibility.

---

### **1. Using your HSA/FSA for Peloton Hardware** 

1. **Choose “Pay with HSA/FSA funds” at checkout**
   - When buying a Peloton Bike, Bike+, or Tread, select the option to pay with HSA/FSA. You will be redirected to the Truemed website.
2. **Take a brief health survey**
   - Answer questions about your HSA/FSA and your health. This helps determine if you meet eligibility requirements for a Letter of Medical Necessity to be issued.
3. **Complete checkout through Truemed**
   - You may be able to pay with your HSA/FSA card directly. If you must use a personal credit/debit card, you can then request reimbursement from your HSA/FSA plan after checkout, provided your purchase is approved.
4. **Receive your Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)**
   - A licensed medical provider will review your responses. If you qualify, Truemed sends you an LMN that states why the Peloton equipment is medically necessary for you. If not, you can cancel your order any time before delivery.

---

### **2. Using your HSA/FSA for Peloton Memberships**

Your monthly Peloton Membership fee may also be eligible for HSA/FSA coverage. Here’s how:

1. **Obtain an LMN that specifically references your Membership by taking the clinical intake questionnaire [here](https://app.truemed.com/qualify/tm_qual_xdk8fitkyw).**
2. **Attempt Payment with HSA/FSA Card**
   - Add your HSA/FSA card to your Peloton Membership billing. If it processes successfully, you can continue using HSA/FSA funds.
3. **If Card Is Declined**
   - Use a regular credit/debit card to avoid interruption of service.
   - Submit a reimbursement claim to your HSA/FSA administrator according to their instructions (often once per calendar year). Make sure to keep your LMN and receipts.

---

### **3. Additional Resources**

- **Contact Your HSA/FSA Provider**\
  For questions about coverage, reimbursement procedures, or plan-specific requirements, reach out directly to your HSA/FSA provider.
- **Truemed Support**\
  Contact Truemed support via [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) for help with the health survey, LMN questions, or assistance with your order.
- **Peloton Support**\
  If you have billing issues or questions about Peloton Membership, Peloton Support can help.

---

### **Summary**

Peloton equipment and memberships can qualify for HSA/FSA reimbursement when supported by a valid Letter of Medical Necessity. By choosing “Pay with HSA/FSA funds” at checkout and completing Truemed’s brief health survey, you’ll learn if you’re eligible to use HSA/FSA for your purchase. Always confirm your plan’s requirements with your HSA/FSA administrator and keep any required documentation on hand for potential reimbursement.
