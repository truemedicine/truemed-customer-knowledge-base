If there’s a health and wellness brand, merchant, or studio you’d like to use your HSA or FSA dollars with, we’re always open to expanding our network-but there’s a process involved.

### Step 1: The Merchant Must Become a Truemed Partner

Truemed can only support HSA/FSA purchases and issue Letters of Medical Necessity (LMNs) for products and services sold by approved partners. This ensures:

- We’ve verified that their offerings meet IRS eligibility standards
- We can provide compliant documentation for your reimbursement or card payment
- The process is seamless for both the customer and the merchant

### Step 2: Encourage the Brand to Apply

If the merchant you want to use isn’t on our eligible list, we recommend sending them to:

👉 www.truemed.com/partners

This is our merchant application page. From there, brands can:

- Learn about Truemed's eligibility criteria
- Apply to join our platform
- Start the onboarding process if they qualify

Many brands join because their customers ask for it — so your recommendation really helps.
