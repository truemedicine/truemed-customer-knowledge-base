## How to Reach Us

Our support team is available 7 days a week to help with any questions about your Truemed account, LMN status, HSA/FSA qualification, or the reimbursement process.

### Customer Support

Email us at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) and our team will get back to you as quickly as possible.

We don't currently offer phone support, but our team is more than happy to assist over email.

### Merchant Support

If you're a merchant with questions about integrating with Truemed or managing your account, reach out to [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).

## Questions About Your Order

For questions about order status, delivery timelines, order cancellations, or the product itself, please contact the merchant you purchased from directly. Truemed handles the HSA/FSA payment processing, but fulfillment and shipping are managed entirely by the merchant.
