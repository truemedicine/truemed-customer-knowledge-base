The Truemed marketplace at [truemed.com](https://truemed.com) is where over 1.5 million people discover health products and brands they can pay for with their HSA or FSA. It's a place for browsing, learning, and finding the right merchant — not for purchasing. Once you find something you want, you click through to the merchant's site to buy, and that's where Truemed's integration comes into play.

This article gives you a high-level orientation to the marketplace: what it is, how it's organized, what you'll find there, and how the flow from discovery to purchase works.

## How the marketplace works

Truemed.com is a discovery and education resource. Its job is to help you find health products that are likely eligible for HSA and FSA spending, connect you with the brands that sell them, and give you enough context to shop confidently.

Here's the basic flow:

1. **Browse the marketplace.** You can explore by health category, search for a specific brand, or scroll through featured merchants on the homepage.
2. **Click through to the merchant.** When you find a product or brand you're interested in, you click a link that takes you directly to the merchant's own website.
3. **Shop and check out on the merchant's site.** Your purchase happens on the merchant's site, not on truemed.com. Your order, shipping, and fulfillment all come from the merchant directly.
4. **Truemed activates at checkout.** The merchant has integrated Truemed into their checkout experience. That's where the HSA/FSA payment option, the qualification survey, and any applicable Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) come in.

The key thing to understand is that **truemed.com itself is not a store.** It's the front door to a network of partner merchants who accept HSA and FSA payments through Truemed's checkout integration.

## Browsing eligible products and categories

The marketplace organizes merchants into health categories, which makes it easy to browse by the type of product you're looking for rather than searching for specific brands.

**Current health categories on the marketplace:**

- **Fitness Equipment** — Cardio machines, strength training, bikes, and more (Peloton, Tonal, Rogue Fitness, Canyon)
- **Health Tech** — Wearables, fitness trackers, and continuous monitors (WHOOP, Oura, Garmin, Meta)
- **Sleep** — Mattresses, smart beds, and sleep tracking devices (Eight Sleep, Purple, Nectar, Saatva)
- **Supplements** — Protein, vitamins, and metabolic health products (AG1, Thorne, ARMRA, Dose)
- **Red Light and Recovery** — Saunas, red light therapy, and recovery tools (Mito Red Light, iRESTORE, SaunaBox)
- **Adaptive Footwear** — Orthopedic and supportive footwear (KURU, Orthofeet, Flux)
- **Home Health** — Air purifiers, ergonomic furniture, and home health gear (Blueair, Secretlab, UPLIFT Desk)
- **Gyms and Studios** — Fitness memberships and studio passes (ClassPass, Barry's)

Each category has its own landing page showing all the brands within that space. From the homepage, horizontal carousels let you preview options in each category and tap through to a full "See all" view.

**How eligibility works by category:** These categories represent areas that commonly qualify under HSA and FSA guidelines when there is a documented medical need. Eligibility is not automatic for any product. Whether a specific purchase qualifies depends on your individual health information and the assessment of an independent, licensed clinician through the Truemed qualification process. Two customers purchasing the same product may receive different outcomes.

## Partner merchant directory

A Truemed partner merchant is a brand that has integrated Truemed into their checkout flow, enabling their customers to pay with HSA or FSA funds. Being listed on truemed.com means the merchant has an active integration with Truemed.

**Finding partners:**

- **Homepage featured merchants** — The homepage highlights a rotating selection of popular and featured brands.
- **Shop All page** — The full partner directory gives you a grid of every listed merchant, organized for easy scanning. You can reach it from the navigation or from any category page.
- **Search bar** — If you have a brand in mind, the search bar at the top of the marketplace lets you look up merchants by name or product type.
- **Category pages** — Each health category page shows all the partner merchants within that space.

**Important to know:** Not every product from a partner merchant is automatically eligible for HSA or FSA payment. Partners integrate Truemed at the checkout level, and eligibility is determined for each purchase based on your health information. Products outside the eligible scope for that merchant's integration may not qualify, even if the brand is listed on the marketplace.

Each merchant card on the marketplace shows the brand logo, a brief description, and a category tag. Clicking a card takes you to that brand's dedicated Truemed page, where you can learn more about the brand and find a direct link to their site.

## Navigating to merchant sites

Getting from the Truemed marketplace to a merchant's checkout is straightforward, but it's worth knowing what to expect when you arrive.

**How the click-through works:**

When you find a merchant or product you'd like to buy, click the "Shop Now" button (or equivalent) on their Truemed page. This takes you directly to the merchant's website, where you can browse their full catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout as normal.

**What to look for at checkout:**

Once you're on the merchant's site, the Truemed experience depends on how the merchant has set up their integration:

- **At-checkout integration (Shopify and OTP merchants):** You'll see Truemed listed as a payment option during checkout. Select it, complete the brief qualification survey, and pay with your HSA or FSA card. To see the Truemed payment option, make sure you're not logged into Shop Pay.
- **Post-purchase integration (Merchant Quals and D2C Quals):** You complete your purchase normally, then receive a link to a qualification survey on the order confirmation page and in a follow-up email. If approved, you'll receive documentation to seek reimbursement from your HSA or FSA administrator.

**If you can't find the Truemed option at checkout:**

- Make sure you're logged out of Shop Pay if you're on a Shopify merchant's site.
- Double-check that the product you're purchasing is within the eligible scope for that merchant's Truemed integration — not all products from a partner merchant are covered.
- If you expect to see a post-purchase survey and haven't received it, check your email (including spam) for a follow-up message from the merchant.
- If you're still stuck, visit [help.truemed.com](https://help.truemed.com) or reach out to Truemed support at support@truemed.com.
