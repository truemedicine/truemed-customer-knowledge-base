Yes, you can use your HSA/FSA benefits on subscription purchases through Truemed. The process works a little differently than a one-time purchase, but the end result is the same: you get a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) that lets you pay or get reimbursed with your tax-advantaged funds.

## What You Need to Know

Because most subscription billing systems are not set up to process HSA/FSA cards directly, Truemed handles subscriptions through a reimbursement-based flow rather than a direct checkout payment. Here is how each path works:

**If you are purchasing a subscription:**

1. Complete your purchase using your regular credit or debit card as you normally would.
2. After checkout, look for a link to the Truemed health survey on your order confirmation page or in your order confirmation email.
3. Complete the Truemed health survey to receive your LMN.
4. Once you have your LMN, submit it to your HSA/FSA administrator for reimbursement of the amount you paid.

You can use this same process to claim reimbursement for recurring subscription charges going forward, as long as your LMN remains valid.

**If you are making a one-time purchase:**

1. At checkout, select Truemed as your payment method.
2. Before doing so, make sure you are logged out of Shop Pay and check out as a guest. This ensures the Truemed payment option appears correctly.
3. Complete the Truemed health survey during checkout to receive your LMN and pay directly with your HSA/FSA card.

The reason these two flows differ comes down to how subscription billing works. Subscription platforms typically charge your card on a recurring schedule, and most of those systems cannot process HSA/FSA cards natively. The survey-and-reimburse approach gives you the same financial benefit while working within the constraints of how subscription payments are processed.

## Final Thoughts

Whether you are signing up for a subscription or making a one-time purchase, Truemed makes it possible to use your HSA/FSA benefits. The path is slightly different depending on how you are buying, but the outcome is the same: you get an LMN that supports your eligibility, and you can pay or be reimbursed using your tax-advantaged funds.

If you are not sure which flow applies to your purchase, or if you run into any trouble along the way, reach out to our support team and we will be happy to help.
