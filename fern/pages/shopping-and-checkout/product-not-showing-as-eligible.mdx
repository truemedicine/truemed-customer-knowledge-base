## Why a product may not appear eligible

HSA/FSA eligibility at checkout is not simply a yes or no per brand. Even within a Truemed partner brand, eligibility is determined at the product level. This means some products from the same brand will show the Truemed HSA/FSA checkout option, and others will not.

There are several common reasons a specific product might not appear eligible when you are shopping:

- **The product hasn't been categorized yet.** The product may actually be eligible, but it hasn't been added to Truemed's system yet. This can happen with new product launches or large catalogs.
- **The product is genuinely ineligible.** Some products from partner brands are accessories, lifestyle items, or otherwise fall outside what the IRS considers a qualified medical expense.
- **You are shopping on a third-party website.** Truemed's integration only works through the brand's direct website. If you are on Amazon, Best Buy, or another retailer, the Truemed option won't appear even if the product itself is eligible.
- **The brand is not a Truemed partner.** If you are on a brand's direct site and see no HSA/FSA option at all, they may not be integrated with Truemed.

Understanding which of these applies to your situation will point you toward the right next step.

## SKU not yet categorized

Truemed works with partner merchants to categorize their product catalog, but large product libraries take time to fully map, and new products are released regularly. In some cases, a product may be genuinely HSA/FSA eligible but simply hasn't been categorized in Truemed's system yet.

**How to tell if this might be the case:**

- The brand is a known Truemed partner and other products from them do show as eligible at checkout.
- The product you want is clearly health-related (for example, a fitness tracker, a supported supplement, or a recovery device).
- There is no HSA/FSA checkout option specifically for your product, even though similar products from the same brand do have one.

**What to do:**

Reach out to Truemed support at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) and share the product name, URL, and the brand you are shopping with. Truemed's team will work with the merchant to evaluate whether the product qualifies and, if so, get it added to the system.

Keep in mind that this process takes some time, as it requires coordination with the merchant and a product-level eligibility review. If your purchase is time-sensitive, you may need to complete it with a regular card and look into reimbursement options later, depending on your HSA/FSA administrator.

## Purchasing on a 3rd party or non-partner website

Truemed's integration is built directly into partner merchants' checkout flows. This means it only works when you are shopping on the brand's own website.

If you find a Truemed-eligible product on a third-party retailer like Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart.com, or another marketplace, you will not see the HSA/FSA option at that retailer's checkout. This is true even if the exact same product is eligible when purchased directly from the brand.

**What to do:**

Go directly to the brand's website to make your purchase. For example:
- If you want an eligible Garmin device, purchase it at **garmin.com** rather than through Amazon or a third-party seller.
- If you are shopping for a supplement brand that partners with Truemed, buy from the brand's direct site.

Shopping directly from the brand ensures you have access to Truemed's checkout and can apply your HSA/FSA funds at the point of sale.

If you have already purchased on a third-party site, you may be able to seek reimbursement directly through your HSA/FSA administrator, depending on the product's eligibility and your administrator's policies. Contact your plan administrator for guidance on that path.

## Ineligible product purchases

Not every product a brand sells qualifies for HSA/FSA use, even from brands that are Truemed partners. Eligibility is determined product by product based on IRS guidelines, not brand by brand.

A good example of this is Garmin. Garmin sells a wide range of products, from health-focused GPS watches and fitness trackers that may be eligible to accessories like watch bands, screen protectors, and car GPS units that are not. The Truemed integration only appears for products that meet the eligibility criteria. If a Garmin product (or any product) does not have an HSA/FSA link or checkout option, it is not eligible through Truemed.

**How to tell if a product is genuinely ineligible:**

- There is no HSA/FSA option at checkout, and the product is an accessory, lifestyle item, or clearly not health-related.
- The product is from a category that does not fall under IRS Section 213(d) qualified medical expenses.

**What to do:**

If the product you want is not eligible, you have a few options:

- **Find an eligible alternative.** The brand may carry a similar product that does qualify. Look for products in the same category that show the HSA/FSA checkout option.
- **Purchase with a regular card.** You can always buy an ineligible product using a standard debit or credit card. Your HSA/FSA account does not have to be used for every purchase from a partner brand.
- **Browse the Truemed marketplace.** Visit [truemed.com](https://truemed.com) to explore eligible products across all partner brands and find options that meet your health needs.

## What to do next

Here is a quick summary of next steps based on your situation:

| Situation | What to do |
|---|---|
| Product should be eligible but has no HSA/FSA option | Contact [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) to request SKU categorization |
| Shopping on Amazon, Best Buy, or another third-party site | Go to the brand's direct website to purchase |
| Product exists on a partner brand's site but isn't eligible | Look for an eligible alternative, or purchase with a regular card |
| Not sure if the brand is a Truemed partner | Browse [truemed.com](https://truemed.com) to find confirmed partner brands and eligible products |

If you are unsure which situation applies to you, reaching out to [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) is always a good place to start. Include the product name, a link to the product page, and a description of what you are seeing at checkout. Truemed's support team can confirm whether the product is eligible, whether it's an uncategorized SKU, or whether you need to shop elsewhere.
