---
title: "How Do I Access or Download My Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)_"
---

For security and privacy reasons, **Truemed no longer sends LMNs via email**. Instead, your Letter of Medical Necessity is securely stored in your Truemed account.

### How to Access Your LMN

1. Go to [www.truemed.com](http://www.truemed.com)
2. Click **Log In** in the upper right corner
3. Enter the email you used when completing your clinical intake form
4. Once logged in, navigate to your dashboard to view and download your LMN as a PDF

---

### Why Can’t You Email It to Me?

To protect your health information and comply with privacy standards, Truemed delivers LMNs exclusively through secure, authenticated access. This helps prevent unauthorized access and ensures your documentation is available in one place.

---

### I don't see my LMN when I log into my user portal?

In these cases we recommend the following:

1. Try logging in using incognito mode on your device. 
2. Confirm you are logging in with the same email address you used to request your LMN.
3. It's possible your order has not yet been captured by the merchant. We recommend waiting a couple of hours and checking in again.

If after attempting all of the above you're still not able to view your LMN, please contact [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com). 

---

### Trouble Logging In?

If you're having trouble accessing your account or don’t remember which email you used:

- Email us at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com)
- Include your full name and approximate date you completed the intake form
- Our support team will help you regain access
