If you’re using your HSA or FSA for a recurring subscription or ongoing purchase (like a monthly supplement or coaching program), your Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) must be **renewed annually**.

### Why Do I Need to Renew My LMN?

The IRS requires that LMNs are valid only for the period during which the treating professional has certified a medical need. Most LMNs issued through Truemed are valid for **12 months** from the date of issuance. Once expired, a new LMN must be obtained to continue using your HSA/FSA funds for that product or service.

### How to Renew Your LMN

1. **Check the expiration date** on your current LMN.
2. When you're within 30 days of expiration, **email**[support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com) and request a new clinical intake survey to start the renewal process.
3. Once you complete the updated survey, our clinician network will review it and issue a new LMN if medical necessity is still justified.
4. You’ll receive a confirmation once your LMN is renewed.
