---
title: "JustWorks Quick Guide_ Submitting HSA_FSA Claims through JustWorks"---


This guide outlines the steps for submitting your FSA reimbursement claims via Justworks. Please note that Justworks uses Optum Financial to administer FSAs. For detailed FAQs and further information, refer to the [JustWorks FSA FAQs](https://help.justworks.com/hc/en-us/articles/360004481412-Flexible-Spending-Accounts-Healthcare-FSA-Dependent-Care-FSA-).

---

## Step 1: Access Your Claim Submission Platform

- **Optum Financial Administration:**\
  Justworks utilizes Optum Financial to process FSA claims.
- **Tutorial Video:**\
  View the claim submission process in this [Tutorial Video](https://www.optum.com/library/health-finances/page.hub.file-claim.html).
- **Optum Financial Website:**\
  For additional details, visit the [Optum Financial website](https://www.optum.com/financial-services.html).

---

## Step 2: Prepare Your Reimbursement Claim

When submitting your reimbursement claim, ensure you include the following:

- **Receipt:**\
  Your receipt should clearly show all required details.
- **Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN):**\
  This document substantiates your claim for reimbursement.

---

## Step 3: Follow On-Screen Instructions

- Navigate through the Optum Financial claim submission process.
- Follow all on-screen prompts to enter the required information and upload your documents.

---
