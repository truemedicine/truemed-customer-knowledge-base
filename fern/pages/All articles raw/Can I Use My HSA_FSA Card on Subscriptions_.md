---
title: "Can I Use My HSA_FSA Card on Subscriptions_"---


**Short Answer:**\
Currently, we do not support HSA/FSA card payments for subscriptions.

---

## What You Need to Know

- **Subscription Payments Not Supported:**
  - At this time, subscriptions cannot be processed using an HSA/FSA card via Truemed.
  - If you try to use your HSA/FSA card for a subscription purchase, the payment will likely be declined.
- **Alternative Payment Method:**
  - We recommend using your regular credit or debit card to complete your subscription purchase.
- **Reimbursement Option:**
  - Once your subscription payment is made using a regular card, you can request reimbursement from your HSA/FSA.
  - Reimbursements for subscription expenses can typically be processed at least once per calendar year.

---

## Final Thoughts

While we currently do not support direct HSA/FSA payments for subscriptions, you can still leverage your HSA/FSA funds by reimbursing yourself after making the purchase with your regular card. If you have any questions or need further assistance with the reimbursement process, please [contact our support team](#). We're here to help!
