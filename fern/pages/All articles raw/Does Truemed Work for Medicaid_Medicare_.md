***
title: "Does Truemed Work for Medicaid_Medicare_"---


Generally speaking, Medicaid/Medicare customers do not have actively funded HSA/FSA accounts unless provided by a previous employer. If you are unsure if you have an HSA/FSA account from a previous employer, we recommend reaching out to the employee benefits coordinator at your previous employer to see what benefits you were enrolled in at that time. 

To utilize Truemed, you will need an [active HSA/FSA account](https://www.aetna.com/health-guide/hsa-vs-fsa.html).
