## Key Points

- **Merchant-Specific Availability:**
  - LMNs for minors are available only with select merchants that support this option.
  - This ensures that LMNs are written accurately and comply with all relevant guidelines.
- **Clinical Intake Survey Process:**
  - When completing the clinical intake survey, you will be asked whether you are taking the survey on behalf of yourself or for someone else (e.g., a minor).
  - This helps us ensure that the LMN is tailored correctly to the patient's needs.
- **Compliance Considerations:**
  - Not all merchants offer LMNs for minors due to strict compliance requirements.
  - Our primary goal is to ensure that each LMN is written properly and in line with regulatory standards.
***
## What You Should Do

- **Check with Support:**
  - If you are interested in obtaining an LMN for a minor, please contact our support team.
  - Our team will confirm whether the merchant you are purchasing from supports minor LMNs and guide you through the necessary steps.
- **Proceed Accordingly:**
  - If minor LMNs are supported for your merchant, follow the clinical intake survey instructions to indicate that you are completing the survey on behalf of a minor.
  - If not, our support team will provide you with alternative options or further guidance.
