---
title: "Why Can't I Use My HSA_FSA Card for Some Merchants_"
---

HSA/FSA cards are often restricted to specific merchant account setups. If a merchant does not have the correct payment integrations, HSA/FSA cards can be declined at checkout. 

In some cases, the HSA/FSA cards will still be accepted when a merchant does not have the correct integrations, so you can certainly try to use your HSA/FSA card. That said, if your HSA/FSA card is declined at checkout, you can submit a[claim for reimbursement using these steps](https://truemedicine.notion.site/How-to-submit-for-HSA-FSA-reimbursement-26bb3092be384a48a2e6779a0254a87c).
