### Best Practice: Keep Your Account Open Until All Refunds Are Processed

Before closing an HSA/FSA account, we strongly recommend:

- Waiting until all transactions and potential refunds have cleared
- Reviewing your purchase history in case a refund may be pending
- Confirming with your provider how they handle funds sent to a closed account
***
If you have any questions about the status of your refund or need documentation to show your HSA/FSA provider, feel free to reach out to us at [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com). We're happy to help with anything within our control.
