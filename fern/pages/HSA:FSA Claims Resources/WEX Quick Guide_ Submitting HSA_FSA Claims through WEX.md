## Step 1: Access Your Account

- **Online:**\
  Log in to your Wex online account and navigate to the reimbursement section.
- **Mobile App:**\
  If you prefer the mobile experience, use the Wex Benefits Mobile App to access your account.

---

## Step 2: Initiate Your Claim

- **Select “Reimburse Myself”:**\
  Follow the instructions provided under the “Reimburse Myself” option to begin your claim submission.

---

## Step 3: Submit Your Documentation

When submitting your reimbursement claim, ensure that you include both:

- **Your Receipt:**\
  This must detail the purchase or service information.
- **Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN):**\
  This document substantiates your claim.
