title: "My Reimbursement Claim Was Denied... Now What_"---


If you receive an alert of a denied claim—don’t worry! We are here to help you navigate next steps.

### **1. Gather Key Details**

To best assist you, please compile the following information:

- **Denial Notice**
  - A screenshot or copy of the denial notice.
  - The **denial reason** clearly stated (or summarized if lengthy).
  - The **denial code**, if one is provided.
- **Your HSA/FSA Administrator Information**
  - The name of your administrator (if not clearly shown on the denial notice).

### **2. Send Us What You Have**

Once you have this information compiled, please email [**support@truemed.com**](mailto:support@truemed.com). Include all the above details (denial notice, administrator name, reason, etc.) in the email body or as attachments. The more details you can provide, the better we can help.

### **3. Next Steps: Appeal Support**

Based on the information you send, our team will do the following:

1. **Review Your Documentation**
   - Our support specialists will examine the denial reason and any codes provided. This helps us understand the root cause (e.g., missing documentation, coverage issue, plan limitations).
2. **Recommend Next Steps**
   - We’ll advise you on any additional documentation that might strengthen an appeal—such as an updated Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) or receipt.

### **4. Common Reasons for Denial**

- **Insufficient Documentation**: Your plan may require more detailed medical records, an LMN that specifically covers your item, or receipts with itemized details.
- **Clerical Error**: Incorrect codes or missing information often lead to an automatic denial.

### **5. How We Help**

Truemed’s mission is to simplify the process of using your HSA/FSA for eligible products and services. If your claim is denied, we can:

- **Clarify Next Steps**: We’ll explain your plan’s terminology and point you to the right resources for an appeal.
- **Update or Edit LMNs**: If needed, we can help you request updated medical documentation.
- **Monitor Progress**: Once you submit an appeal, we can check in with you on any additional requests your plan may have.

### **6. Additional Tips & Resources**

- **Contact Your HSA/FSA Administrator**: Ask about specific appeal deadlines or unique documentation requirements.
- **Keep Original Documents**: Maintain digital or hard copies of all receipts and LMNs; you may need them again.
