## Step 1: Access the Gusto Benefits Card Manager Portal

- **Portal URL:**
  Navigate to the [Gusto Benefits Card Manager Portal](https://gusto.wealthcareportal.com/Page/Home).
***
## Step 2: Submit Your Reimbursement Claim

- **Include Required Documentation:**
  - Your receipt for the purchase
  - Your Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)
- **Follow On-Screen Instructions:**
  The portal will guide you through the steps necessary to submit your claim.
***
This quick guide is designed to help you efficiently submit your HSA/FSA reimbursement claims through Gusto. For further assistance, please consult the Gusto FAQs or reach out to support@truemed.com
