## Option 1: Submit a Claim Online

### Step 1: Access the Online Portal

- **Portal URL:**
  Visit the [Payflex Online Portal](https://www.mypayflex.com/SignIn/SignIn/Index/member) and log in using your credentials.

### Step 2: Initiate the Claim Submission Process

- **Navigation:**
  Locate the claim submission or reimbursement section within the portal.
- **Action:**
  Follow the on-screen instructions to begin your claim.

### Step 3: Upload Required Documentation

- **Receipt:**
  Ensure your receipt includes all necessary details.
- **Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN):**
  Attach your LMN to substantiate your claim.

### Step 4: Finalize and Submit

- **Review:**
  Double-check that all information and documents are accurate.
- **Submit:**
  Complete the submission process as instructed on the portal.
***
## Option 2: Submit a Claim via the Mobile App

### Step 1: Access the Payflex Mobile App

- **Action:**
  Download and open the Payflex Mobile app on your smartphone.
- **Login:**
  Sign in with your Payflex credentials.

### Step 2: Initiate the Claim Process

- **Navigation:**
  Navigate to the claim submission or reimbursement section in the app.
- **Action:**
  Follow the guided steps to input your claim details.

### Step 3: Upload Required Documentation

- **Receipt:**
  Ensure your receipt includes the required information.
- **Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN):**
  Attach the LMN along with your receipt.

### Step 4: Review and Submit

- **Review:**
  Confirm that all details are correct and both documents are attached.
- **Submit:**
  Finalize your claim by following the app’s submission process.
***
**Important Note:**
When submitting reimbursements, be sure to include your receipt and Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity. This is essential for ensuring your claim is processed smoothly.
***
This quick guide is designed to help you efficiently submit your FSA claims through Payflex. For further assistance, please consult Payflex support resources or contact support@truemed.com
