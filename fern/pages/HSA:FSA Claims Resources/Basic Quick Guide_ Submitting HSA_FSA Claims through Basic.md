This guide outlines the steps to submit your HSA and FSA reimbursement claims through Basic. When submitting reimbursements, be sure to include your receipt and Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity.

---

## For HSA Claims

**Submission Methods:**

- **BASIC CDA System:**\
  Log in and submit your receipts and documentation via the [BASIC CDA System](https://cda.basiconline.com/login).
- **BASIC Benefits App:**\
  Alternatively, use the [BASIC Benefits App](https://www.basiconline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/CDA-108-BASIC-Benefits-App.pdf).

**Reimbursement Details:**\
Claims will be processed as reimbursement to MyCash, which is BASIC’s post-tax reimbursement account linked to participants’ BASIC Cards.

---

## For FSA Claims

**Submission Method:**

- **BASIC Benefits App:**\
  Submit your FSA claim through the [BASIC Benefits App](https://www.basiconline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/CDA-108-BASIC-Benefits-App.pdf).

**Reimbursement Details:**\
Your FSA claim will be processed as a reimbursement to MyCash, BASIC’s post-tax reimbursement account linked to your BASIC Card.

---
