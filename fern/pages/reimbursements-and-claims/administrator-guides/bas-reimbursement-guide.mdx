title: Benefit Allocation Systems (BAS) Reimbursement Guide
description: Step-by-step instructions for submitting a Truemed HSA/FSA reimbursement claim through Benefit Allocation Systems (BAS).
---

In all cases, you will need two documents: the **receipt** from your purchase and the **Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)** issued by Truemed.

## Required documentation

- Receipt
- Truemed Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)

## Submission steps

1. Log in to the BAS participant portal at [myenroll.com](https://www.myenroll.com/web_projects/MainHomePage/Login.aspx).
2. Navigate to the FSA reimbursement section.
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to begin your claim.
4. Upload your receipt and your Truemed LMN.
5. Review all information and submit.

For additional guidance, refer to BAS's "Guide to Your FSA Reimbursements" available at [basusa.com](https://basusa.com).
