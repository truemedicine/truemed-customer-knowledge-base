A Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) is the document that allows you to use your HSA or FSA funds for eligible purchases through Truemed. Most of the time, your LMN is issued automatically as part of the Truemed checkout process. But there are a few situations where you may need to request one differently, or where an LMN may not be possible. This article walks through each scenario.

## Requesting an LMN for a new purchase

For most purchases through a Truemed partner merchant, getting an LMN is built directly into checkout. Here is how the process works:

1. **Select the Truemed payment option at checkout.** When you reach the payment step, choose Truemed as your payment method.
2. **Complete the clinical intake survey.** You will be prompted to answer a short health questionnaire before your payment is finalized. This survey is required to establish medical necessity.
3. **Your survey is sent to a licensed provider for review.** The provider reviews your responses to determine whether an LMN is appropriate for your situation.
4. **Your LMN is issued after approval.** If the provider approves your request, your LMN is generated and associated with your purchase. You can access it from your [Truemed dashboard](https://app.truemed.com).

The key requirement is that the survey must be completed **on or before the day of purchase**. The LMN date is tied to when you complete the survey, and your HSA or FSA administrator will verify that this date aligns with your purchase date.

If you have questions about your survey results or LMN status, see [LMN Status & Troubleshooting](/lmns/lmn-status-and-troubleshooting).

## Requesting an LMN for a past purchase (retroactive)

We hear this question often, and we want to be upfront with you: **Truemed is not able to issue an LMN for a purchase that was already made outside of Truemed checkout.**

Here is why. In order for an LMN to be valid, the clinical intake survey must be completed before or on the day of purchase. This is not a policy we created arbitrarily -- it is an IRS compliance requirement. The survey establishes a patient-provider relationship, and that relationship must exist at the time of the transaction. Because of this, we are not able to backdate a letter of medical necessity to a time prior to the establishment of that relationship.

If your purchase was made through the merchant's standard checkout instead of Truemed, the survey was never completed, and there is no qualifying documentation to support an LMN for that transaction.

**What you can do next:**

- **If this was a one-time purchase:** You can reach out to the merchant to see if they can process a refund, which would allow you to repurchase using Truemed at checkout and go through the proper qualification process.
- **For future purchases:** To make sure your purchase is eligible, select the Truemed payment option at checkout and complete the clinical intake survey before your payment is finalized.

We know this can be frustrating, especially if you were not aware of the Truemed option at checkout. For future purchases, keep an eye out for the Truemed payment option wherever you shop.

## Requesting an LMN for a non-partner or third-party merchant

Truemed issues LMNs specifically for purchases made through our partner merchant network. If a merchant is not a Truemed partner, we generally cannot issue a standard LMN for products purchased there, because we have not verified the merchant's products for HSA/FSA eligibility.

**There is one exception: gym memberships.** If you have a gym membership at a facility that is not a Truemed partner, we may be able to issue a generic LMN for gym membership expenses, provided that medical necessity is established through the clinical intake survey. This LMN can then be submitted to your HSA or FSA administrator along with your itemized receipts, following your plan's submission guidelines. Your HSA/FSA administrator makes the final determination on whether the reimbursement is approved.

For all other non-partner merchants:

- We are not able to issue LMNs for services like coaching, physical therapy, or diagnostics purchased outside of our partner network.
- If there is a merchant you love and want to shop through Truemed, encourage them to apply at [truemed.com/partners](https://truemed.com/partners).

If you are unsure whether your situation qualifies for an LMN, reach out to our support team and we can help you figure out the best path forward.

## Requesting an LMN for a spouse or dependent

HSA and FSA funds can be used for qualifying medical expenses for your spouse and tax dependents, not just yourself. If you are purchasing a product for a family member, you may still be able to use your HSA or FSA funds, provided the purchase meets the eligibility requirements.

**To request an LMN that covers a dependent:**

- The clinical intake survey should reflect the medical needs of the **person the product is for**, even if the account holder is the one making the purchase.
- When completing the survey, answer the health questions based on your dependent's condition, not your own.
- Contact our support team if you need help getting a survey link that is set up correctly for a dependent.

**IRS rules on dependent eligibility:**

- You can use HSA funds for a spouse and anyone you claim as a dependent on your federal tax return.
- FSA funds follow similar rules, and in many cases can also be used for a child up to age 26, even if they are not claimed as a dependent.
- Your dependent does not need to be covered under your health plan to qualify.

If you have questions about whether a specific family member qualifies, we recommend confirming with your HSA or FSA plan administrator before completing a purchase.

## Getting a survey link for a new product

If you want to use Truemed for a product you have not purchased before -- or if your existing survey link has expired -- you can request a new survey link through our support team.

Common reasons you might need a new survey link:

- You want to purchase a product from a Truemed partner that you have not used before and need to complete a new intake survey.
- Your previous survey link expired before you had a chance to complete it.
- You are looking to add a new product category that requires a separate qualification.

**To get a new survey link:**

Reach out to our support team with the following information:
- The merchant or product you are looking to purchase
- Your email address associated with your Truemed account

Our team will get you a fresh survey link so you can complete the intake process and move forward with your purchase.
