## Gym & Fitness Studio Memberships with an LMN

Gym and fitness studio memberships may be eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) issued by a licensed clinical provider through Truemed.

## Partner Gyms & Studios

Truemed has partnerships with a number of gyms and fitness studios, including:

- [solidcore]
- 24 Hour Fitness
- Barry's
- ClassPass
- Club Pilates
- CorePower Yoga
- SoulCycle

Browse the full list of available partners here:

[View partner gyms & studios](https://www.truemed.com/shop/gym-studios)

Select your gym or studio, complete the health survey, and a licensed clinical provider on Truemed's network will review your information. If you qualify, your LMN will be issued.

## Don't See Your Gym?

If your gym or studio isn't listed as a Truemed partner, you can still request a generic gym membership LMN by completing this health survey:

[Complete the health survey](https://app.truemed.com/qualify/tm_qual_03dz17gzzf)

This covers memberships at gyms and studios not currently in the Truemed partner network.

## Submitting Your Reimbursement

Once your LMN is approved and issued by a clinical provider:

1. Download your LMN from your [Truemed dashboard](https://www.truemed.com).
2. Gather your purchase receipt from your gym or studio.
3. Log into your HSA/FSA administrator's portal.
4. Submit both your LMN and receipt as documentation for your reimbursement claim.

For step-by-step instructions specific to your HSA/FSA administrator, see [Submitting Your Claim by Administrator](/reimbursements-claims/submitting-your-claim-by-administrator).
