***
title: "Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Preapproved"---


 1.  **What is the return or exchange policy?** All orders placed through the website are final; we do not allow returns or exchanges. However, if you did not receive your order or received a defective or damaged item, please contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com) within 60 days of placing your order.
 2. **Are there any limits to what I can order?** We reserve the right to limit the quantity of certain items. If you have questions, please contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com) within 60 days of placing your order.
 3. **Do product images match what will be delivered?** Most images displayed match the product that will be delivered; however, please review the product description for the most accurate details.
 4. **Where can I get a copy of my order receipt?** You will receive an order receipt upon completing your purchase. If you have an account, log in and download a copy from your order history. If you do not have an account, contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com) and we will provide a copy.
 5. **Will all the items in my order ship together?** We attempt to ship all items together, but this is not always possible. In some cases, your order may be split into multiple shipments, which may arrive on the same or different days.
 6. **How long will it take for my order to arrive?** Most orders are delivered within 3–5 business days after being placed. If you have questions about your order status, please contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com).
 7. **Why did I receive a notice that my order was cancelled?** If an item in your order was out of stock and could not be fulfilled, your order may be cancelled. In such cases, your card will be automatically credited for the cost of the item and any applicable taxes.
 8. **When will I receive a credit for a cancelled order?** We will process a credit to your account within one business day of the cancellation. Please note that your financial institution may take additional time to reflect the adjustment. If you have questions, contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com).
 9. **Can I track my order delivery?** Yes. Your order will include a tracking number that can be used to monitor delivery status. You can access the tracking number by logging in to your account and viewing your order history.
10. **Are all items FSA eligible?** Yes. We curate the website to include only items eligible under the SIGIS list of FSA-allowable products. If you have questions about product eligibility, please contact us at [marketplace-support@truemed.com](mailto:marketplace-support@truemed.com).
