---
title: "What Happens If My HSA_FSA Refund Was Sent to a Closed Account_"---


If you requested a refund for a purchase made with your HSA or FSA card and that account is now closed, unfortunately, **Truemed is not able to assist further** once the refund has been issued. This can be a frustrating situation, so here’s what you need to know.

### Refunds Must Go Back to the Original Payment Method

- **Truemed is only able to refund the original card used for the purchase.**
- This is a hard requirement from our payment processor and ensures compliance with IRS regulations.
- We cannot issue a refund to a different card, reprocess the transaction, or send a check or alternative form of reimbursement.

### What If the Account Is Closed?

- If the HSA or FSA account is no longer active, **the funds are still sent to that account**.
- At that point, **it is the consumer’s responsibility** to contact their former HSA/FSA provider and request access to the returned funds.
- In most cases, if you no longer have access to the account, your provider may be able to redirect or reclaim the refund depending on their policies — but this is entirely outside of Truemed’s control.

### Why Can’t Truemed Help in This Case?

- Once the refund is issued to the original payment method, **the funds leave our system** and go back through the payment processor to the financial institution that issued your HSA/FSA card.
- **We do not have access to closed account systems** and cannot recall or reroute those funds.

---

### Best Practice: Keep Your Account Open Until All Refunds Are Processed

Before closing an HSA/FSA account, we strongly recommend:

- Waiting until all transactions and potential refunds have cleared
- Reviewing your purchase history in case a refund may be pending
- Confirming with your provider how they handle funds sent to a closed account
